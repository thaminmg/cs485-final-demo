K12 Mobile Learning App

Sep. 2020 - Dec. 2020

Flutter, Firebase Authentication, Firestore Database and Storage

  • Developed and launched a mobile learning application for K12 students to efficiently search and access over 20 years of Myanmar matriculation exam questions and answers.
  • Built a minimum viable product by using the Flutter UI toolkit and Firebase Backend-as-a-Service, following the clean architecture repository pattern.
  • Published the app on the Google Play Store, achieving over 1,200 downloads with an average rating of 4.8 stars. https://bit.ly/31ROGQv

E-commerce App


The "E-commerce App" is a dynamic online shopping platform designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for customers. It allows users to browse and purchase products from a brand, with features that enhance convenience and accessibility. With a focus on user-friendly design and secure transactions, this app aims to revolutionize the way customers shop online.

E-commerce Dashboard


This is a powerful admin panel designed to manage and monitor an e-commerce platform. It offers tools for tracking sales, managing inventory, and analyzing customer behavior. With its intuitive interface, this dashboard helps businesses optimize their operations and enhance the shopping experience for users.

Jewelry Production and Management


The "Jewelry Production and Management" dashboard is a specialized tool designed for jewelry shop owners to streamline their operations. It provides features to manage inventory, track production processes, and monitor sales performance. With an intuitive interface, this dashboard helps businesses improve efficiency and enhance customer service in the jewelry industry.

Bus Station Ticketing


The system is an advanced solution for electronic receipt printing and management at bus stations. It enables efficient ticket processing, ensuring quick and accurate transactions for customers. This system simplifies the management of ticket sales and enhances the overall travel experience for passengers.

Association


The "Association" project focuses on creating a centralized contact book for managing member information. It allows users to easily store, organize, and access contact details for individuals within the association. This tool enhances communication and simplifies networking among members, improving overall efficiency.
